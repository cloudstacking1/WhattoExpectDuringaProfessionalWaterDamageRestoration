The True Cost of Neglect

Many homeowners underestimate how quickly water damage can escalate. Moisture hidden behind drywall or under flooring is a mold incubator. Once mold takes hold, it not only degrades surfaces but can pose serious health risks—especially for children, the elderly, and individuals with respiratory conditions. Swift restoration prevents mold and saves you money in the long run by addressing damage early.
